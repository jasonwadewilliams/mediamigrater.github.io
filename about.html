About Me

Hi, my name is Jason and I picked up this hobby several years ago when converting my own family home videos.
I have since converted all of my extended families home videos so that we can all enjoy them in a modern format
that is easy to share. I picked up a bunch of different pieces of retro technology along the way and I started
offering my services at a super low cost just as a way to fund my ongoing hobby. Please feel free to reach out
with any questions or inquaries and I would love to help preserve your family memories./p>

Pricing:
I charge a basic $5 per tape/disc. I can provide a download link free of charge but if you want
it saved to a phisical usb drive I charge the cost of the usb drive in addition to the cost of conversion.
